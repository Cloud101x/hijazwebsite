# PRD · Hijaz Studio (Agency)

## Problem
An elite Nigerian product engineering studio needs a landing site that earns trust from Africa-based fintech CTOs **and** signals export-grade craft to global clients. The current generic "consulting site" template undersells the work and reads as a junior team.

## Target audience
1. **Primary** — VPEng / CTO at Nigerian fintechs and AI startups evaluating senior engineering partners.
2. **Secondary** — Global founders looking to hire an African studio for cost + quality leverage.
3. **Tertiary** — Senior engineers and designers considering joining the studio.

## Outcomes
- Surface that the studio is **senior** (no juniors) and **fast** (4.6× velocity) within 6 seconds of landing.
- Convert qualified leads to a brief submission — at least one CTA visible at every scroll position.
- Establish brand authority via Africa-first signals (Lagos clock, ₦ pricing, African partner logos, NDPR / CBN compliance copy).

## Non-goals
- Not a blog or content hub.
- Not optimised for SEO at MVP — direct outreach and press drives traffic.
- No live booking calendar — CTA opens a brief form, sales follows up.

## Success metrics
| Metric | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Brief submissions / month | 12+ |
| Lighthouse perf (mobile) | ≥ 85 |
| 90th percentile TTI | < 4s on Naija 4G |
| Time on page (median) | ≥ 90s |
