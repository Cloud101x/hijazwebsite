# PRD · Bedrock Works (Construction)

## Problem
Nigerian construction firms with billion-naira track records are routinely losing bids to Chinese SOEs and Lebanese conglomerates because their digital presence reads as cheap. Mid-tier procurement officers Google a firm before they call — and most local firms show up looking like a 2009 WordPress site. We need a website that signals **scale, safety record, and seriousness** within 4 seconds.

## Target audience
1. **Government procurement officers** (FCDA, NNPC, ministries) shortlisting tier-1 contractors
2. **Multinational developers** entering the Nigerian market (Eko Atlantic, Dangote Group, Maersk)
3. **Private estate owners** commissioning a townhouse or villa
4. **Sub-contractors and material suppliers** vetting upstream partners

## Outcomes
- Within 4 seconds: visitor sees scale (180+ projects · ₦620B built · 14 active sites · 0 fatalities)
- Project gallery filters by trade so an Eko Atlantic-style buyer can find a high-rise reference, an FCT buyer can find a roadworks reference
- Quote request is one click from the navbar — and obvious in the hero
- Signals certifications (ISO 9001, ISO 45001, OHSAS) without making them the headline

## Non-goals
- No public RFQ portal — quote requests are routed to the BD team
- No login / portal for clients in MVP
- No blog (a "Press" page is enough)
