# PRD · Maison Lagos (Real Estate)

## Problem
Nigerian luxury real estate is mostly listed on portals built for ₦20M apartments. A ₦4B Banana Island villa shouldn't appear in the same UI as a starter flat. Buyers and sellers at the top of the market want **discretion, brokers they know by name, and a presentation that matches the price.** They also want a quick way to size a mortgage without filling out a form.

## Target audience
1. **Diaspora returnees / global Nigerians** buying a primary residence in Lagos or Abuja
2. **High-net-worth families** moving between properties or building a portfolio
3. **Family offices** acquiring on behalf of beneficiaries
4. **Selected international buyers** (London, Dubai, Houston) entering the Nigerian market

## Outcomes
- Position the brokerage as **quiet** — most inventory is off-market, public listings are curated
- Make the **price** of every listing impossible to miss (it's a luxury good — price is the point)
- Mortgage calculator: tell a buyer in 60 seconds what a ₦1.6B house actually costs monthly
- The three principal brokers are the human face — buyers should leave the page knowing who to call

## Non-goals
- Not a portal — sellers don't self-list
- No public bidding / auction features
- No virtual-tour viewer in MVP (private viewings are part of the value)

## Success metrics
| Metric | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Private viewing requests / week | ≥ 6 qualified |
| Mortgage-calculator engagements | ≥ 35% of sessions |
| Inbound "off-market" enquiries / month | ≥ 10 |
| Lighthouse perf (mobile) | ≥ 86 |
